Utkarsh Agarwal

Founder

Utkarsh Agarwal is the founder of the 3d Youth Films Group (3dYF Group) which was founded by him in the year 2016. Since then he has been working with the group with full determination. Besides 3d Youth Films Group he also has other personal venture which includes CROP-SHOP and cfk.

This right attitude towards work and managing skills has helped him a lot towards motivating the youth to join him in such works. Now, he and his team is completely focused and determined to empower the society.
