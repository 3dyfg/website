SOCIAL DRIVES

We have organised more than 20 social drives across thr city for the welfare of the society and took a step forward towards a better tomorrow.

Discover More

Sanitary Drive

Series of drive which includes distribution of sanitary pads and educating women about personal hygiene. 2 such drives have been executed till date.

Donation Drive

The drive includes the distribution of clothes, stationary items, food products to the needy people. Till now we have executed 3 such drives.

Educational Drive

The moto of the drive was to educate school students about the harmful effects of plastic use and how we can overcome this at personal level.

Women Education Drive

The drive involved distribution of sanitary pads and majorly focusing on educating and empowering of village women. It was in collaboration with a college student chapter.

Plantation Drive

The drive involved plantation of trees and educating village school students regarding the same. 50 such trees were planted on that single day.

Health Camp

This camp was organised to provide village people with the free medical checkup and some medicines. It was organised in collaboration with Bharat Vikas Parishad.
